Conduction

How does heat flow along a solid? Atoms at the heated end gain kinetic energy and vibrate more violently, colliding with neighbours. In metals, free electrons also drift rapidly to transfer energy.

Curriculum Concept

Radiation

Radiation transfers heat by IR electromagnetic waves — no medium required, works in vacuum. Dull black surfaces absorb and emit well; shiny silver surfaces reflect IR and absorb poorly.

Greenhouse Effect

Short-wavelength radiation enters easily through glass. Warm inner objects absorb it and re-emit long-wavelength infrared, which is trapped by the glass.
