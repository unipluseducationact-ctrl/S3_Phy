1.2 Key Terms
Incident ray （入射光線）: Light ray approaching the surface
Reflected ray （反射光線）: Light ray leaving the surface
Normal （法線）: Perpendicular line to the surface at point of incidence
Angle of incidence （入射角）: Angle between incident ray and normal
Angle of reflection （反射角）: Angle between reflected ray and normal
Point of incidence （入射點）: Point where light hits the surface

8. Important Concepts
8.1 Virtual vs Real Image
Formed by extensions of reflected rays
Cannot be projected on screen
Appears behind mirror
Always erect
Formed by actual convergence of reflected rays
Can be projected on screen
Appears in front of mirror
Usually inverted
8.2 Lateral Inversion
Left and right are reversed in plane mirror
Top and bottom remain the same
Example:
Text appears reversed (mirror writing)
Clock appears to run backwards
Word "AMBULANCE" written reversed on front of ambulance
Can be read correctly in rear-view mirror
